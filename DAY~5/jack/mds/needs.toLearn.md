To master Angular as a professional developer, focus on these core mechanisms:

* **Component Communication:** `@Input()`, `@Output()`, `Template Variables`, `@ViewChild()`, and shared RxJS Services.
* **Routing & Navigation:** Lazy Loading, Route Guards (`canActivate`), Path Parameters, and Query Parameters.
* **State Management:** Component State, Services with `BehaviorSubject`, and Global Stores (NgRx or Signals-based state).
* **Reactive Programming:** RxJS Operators (`map`, `filter`, `switchMap`), Observables, and Subject variants.
* **Angular Signals:** Signal state, `computed` values, `effect` reactions, and Signal-based inputs/outputs.
* **Dependency Injection (DI):** Hierarchical Injectors, Custom Providers (`provideIn: 'root'`), and the `inject()` function.
* **Forms Management:** Template-driven Forms vs. Reactive Forms, Custom Validators, and `ControlValueAccessor`.
* **Directives & Pipes:** Custom Structural/Attribute Directives and Custom Pipes for data transformation.
* **Performance Optimization:** `OnPush` Change Detection Strategy, TrackBy functions, and Server-Side Rendering (SSR).
* **Testing:** Unit Testing with Jasmine/Karma (or Jest) and End-to-End testing.
